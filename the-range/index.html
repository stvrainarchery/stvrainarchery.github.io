The range is open to members only

Saint Vrain Archery Range is located 4.6 miles East of Main Street on Hwy 119 (the new intersection of Pratt/119 and Main) and about 1.8 Miles West of I-25 on Hwy 119 (Exit 240 off I-25.) Look for the red topped building). Turn South on WCR 5. Go straight until you hit the first red gate (marked by the blue line on the map). There is a second gate, also marked in blue, 350 yards from the first gate.

NOTE: All Visitors must be with a member and sign an INSURANCE WAIVER.
